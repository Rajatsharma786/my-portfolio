Featured Projects

Lexi Law Agent

Lexi Law Agent is an AI legal assistant that uses domain-specific embeddings and retrieval to interpret Victorian legislation, generate court forms, and analyze legal documents. The developer ingested and precisely chunked 130+ PDFs and used a Hugging Face sentence-transformers Legal-BERT model for domain-tuned embeddings, improving chunking, retrieval, and reranking accuracy. A multi-agent pipeline combines OCR, ChromaDB vector search, cross-encoder reranking, and Redis caching to deliver low-latency, production-ready LLM responses on Azure.
